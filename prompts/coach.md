## Coaching Style Variants

### Tactical Mastermind
- Expression: calm_focused
- Attire: "Sharp tracksuit with team crest, collar popped"
- Accent: "Holographic tactical board floating nearby, chess-piece energy"
- Pose: "Arms crossed, analytical gaze, slight knowing smile"

### Fiery Motivator
- Expression: shouting
- Attire: "Polo shirt sleeves pushed up, intense energy"
- Accent: "Fire and passion energy, rallying aura"
- Pose: "Fist clenched, leaning forward with intensity, motivational energy"

### Player Developer
- Expression: warm_focused
- Attire: "Training gear, approachable appearance"
- Accent: "Growth/nurturing energy, seedling-to-tree motif"
- Pose: "Open posture, encouraging gesture, patient expression"

### Old School
- Expression: stern
- Attire: "Classic coaching jacket, old-fashioned whistle"
- Accent: "Weathered wisdom energy, vintage football motifs"
- Pose: "Standing firm, no-nonsense stance, experienced eyes"
