## NPC Role Variants

### Referee
- Attire: "Black referee jersey with yellow trim, collar"
- Accessories: "Silver whistle in mouth, red and yellow cards in breast pocket"
- Accent: "Authority yellow glow, impartial justice energy"
- Pose: "Stern stance, hand raised making a call, no-nonsense"
- Background: "Pitch line markings subtly visible"
- Aura: "#f39c12"

### Scout / Recruiter
- Attire: "Nondescript jacket, baseball cap low, inconspicuous"
- Accessories: "Tablet or notebook with player notes, sunglasses"
- Accent: "Spotlight/searchlight scanning effect, data overlay"
- Pose: "Slightly turned away, observing from distance, analytical"
- Background: "Distant stadium lights, observation post feel"
- Aura: "#7f8c8d"

### Club Director / Administrator
- Attire: "Business casual, polo with club crest"
- Accessories: "Clipboard, lanyard with access badge"
- Accent: "Corporate/organizational energy, hierarchical glow"
- Pose: "Professional stance, clipboard held, evaluating"
- Background: "Office/facility hints, club branding"
- Aura: "#2c3e50"

### Rival Player
- Attire: "Opposing team jersey, different color scheme"
- Accessories: "Captain armband optional, intense game-ready"
- Accent: "Threatening shadow energy, rival fire"
- Pose: "Confrontational stance, competitive stare-down"
- Background: "Split-screen rival energy, opposing color clash"
- Aura: "#c0392b"

### Sibling
- Attire: "Casual clothes, family resemblance to main character"
- Accessories: "Depends on personality — book, ball, phone"
- Accent: "Family bond energy, complementary color to protagonist"
- Pose: "Natural casual pose, personality-appropriate"
- Background: "Home/family environment hints"
- Aura: "#1abc9c"
