## Parent Type Variants

### Karen (Overbearing/Aggressive)
- Expression: "angry, veins visible on forehead, intense glare"
- Attire: "Designer sideline outfit, oversized sunglasses pushed up"
- Accessories: "Phone in hand ready to record, pointing finger"
- Accent: "Red pressure energy, steam/heat distortion"
- Pose: "Leaning forward aggressively, finger pointing, mouth open shouting"
- Aura: "#e74c3c"

### Brad (Rival/Competitive)
- Expression: "smug smirk, condescending look, arms crossed"
- Attire: "Expensive coaching brand gear, team dad vibes"
- Accessories: "Energy drink, coaching whistle he bought himself"
- Accent: "Competitive fire energy, one-upmanship aura"
- Pose: "Arms crossed, looking down, alpha parent energy"
- Aura: "#e67e22"

### Mike (Supportive/Friend)
- Expression: "genuine warm smile, encouraging eyes"
- Attire: "Comfortable casual wear, team hoodie"
- Accessories: "Coffee cup, folding chair visible"
- Accent: "Warm supportive glow, friendship energy"
- Pose: "Relaxed posture, thumbs up or clapping, genuine enjoyment"
- Aura: "#27ae60"

### Spouse (Partner/Co-parent)
- Expression: "balanced, slightly concerned or warmly supportive"
- Attire: "Casual weekend wear, practical for sideline"
- Accessories: "Snack bag, blanket, sibling management"
- Accent: "Family warmth, balanced energy, grounding presence"
- Pose: "Supportive stance, watching game attentively"
- Aura: "#3498db"
